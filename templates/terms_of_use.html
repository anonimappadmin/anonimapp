Terms of Use

Anonimapp exists to protect voices that need safety — not to harbor abuse. By using this platform, you agree not to use it for any unlawful, exploitative, or malicious purpose. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Harassment, stalking, or doxxing
  • Threats of violence or terrorism
  • Child exploitation or abuse
  • Organized crime or fraud
  • Spreading hateful or discriminatory content

Legal Compliance

While Anonimapp is designed with privacy as a core value, we operate under the laws of our hosting jurisdiction. If legally compelled by a valid court order or lawful request from authorities, we may be required to disclose specific information — to the extent we have any.

However, since we do not collect personal identifiers, store IP addresses, or track user activity, our ability to provide meaningful data is extremely limited by design.

In other words: we don’t keep what we don’t want to be asked for.

Data Handling

  • Messages self-destruct after 3 views or 30 seconds (whichever comes first).
  • Voice messages are auto-deleted after playback.
  • We use localStorage only to help you remember messages you sent. No data is synced or tracked externally.

Jurisdiction

This platform is governed by the laws of the country or region in which it is hosted. If you're accessing Anonimapp from another jurisdiction, it's your responsibility to ensure that your use complies with your local laws.

Responsibility & Reporting

We take misuse seriously. If you believe someone is using Anonimapp to cause harm, please contact us immediately.

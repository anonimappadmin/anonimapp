Our Privacy Commitment

Anonimapp is built to give you a safe place to share anonymously — no sign-ups, no tracking, no strings attached. Here’s what you need to know:

We Don’t Collect Personal Info
  • No emails, phone numbers, or names are collected.
  • No account is needed to use Anonimapp.
No Tracking or Cookies
  • We don’t use cookies to track users.
  • No Google Analytics. No third-party trackers.
Your Messages Stay Private
  • Messages are stored temporarily and self-destruct after 3 views or 30 seconds.
  • Voice messages are deleted permanently after playback.
LocalStorage on Your Device
  • We use localStorage to help you view messages you've sent — only on your device.
  • If you clear browser data or switch devices, those messages won’t be visible anymore.
Compliance
  • We comply with privacy laws like GDPR and CCPA by not collecting personal data.
Hosting & Security
  • Our server is protected using modern security standards.
  • All data is transmitted securely via HTTPS.
  • For even greater anonymity, you can access Anonimapp via privacy-first browsers like Tor.
  • localStorage won't persist in Tor beyond the session — perfect for even stricter privacy.

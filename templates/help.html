Help & Walkthroughs

How to Post an Anonymous Text Message

Go to New Message from the top menu. Type your message, give it an optional subject, and hit Send Anonymous Message. You'll get a shareable link and QR code.

How to Record a Voice Message

Switch to the Voice tab on the New Message page. Tap the mic to record, then send. Share the generated link like a normal message. Only one playback per recipient.

How to Access a Message You've Received

Use the Access Message button or go directly to the shared link. Enter the access key if prompted. Remember: keys can be used only once.

How to View Messages You've Sent

Click on My Messages. We use localStorage to show you what you've sent from your device. If you've cleared your browser or changed devices, they won't appear here.

How to Stay Private

Use Tor for extra privacy. Avoid sharing personally identifiable information in your messages. Your IP isn't tracked, but your device can still give clues.
